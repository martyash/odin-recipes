fried Rice


description

This is a very simple recipe that takes about 20 minutes to cook. You don't need to be an expert

Ingredients

  • Brown rice
  • Chicken thigh fillet
  • 2 Eggs
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Chicken stock
  • Chilli sauce
  • Soy sauce
  1. First add oil to frying pain and let it heat
  2. Set heat to medium
  3. Add vegetables to frying pan and start cooking
  4. Break open 2 eggs into a small bowl and whisk until yolk mixes with white
  5. Add eggs to frying pan
  6. After the eggs begin to get solid, start breaking it into small pieces
  7. Add chicken in and add chilli sauce + soy sauce
  8. Add chicken stock after 1 minute
  9. Add cooked brown rice to the frying pan and start mixing
  10. When meat is cooked properly just prepare onto a plate

Protein milkshake


description

This is a delicious high calorie, high protein milkshake to start off your day.

Ingredients

  • 2 Bananas
  • Milk
  • Protein powder
  • Kefir
  • Peanut butter
  • Steel cut oats (not quick oats)
  • Ice cubes
  1. add a small amount of water, a few ml to the blender bottle, this will prevent clumping in your drink
  2. add milk until you reach 100ml mark
  3. Add 1-2 scoops of protein powder and shake from the top of the bottle until the two current ingredients mix
  4. Break 1 banana into small pieces and add to bottle
  5. Break the second banana and add a spoonful of peanut butter to one of the pieces and add to the bottle
  6. Then add the rest of the pieces into the shake
  7. Add 100g of oats
  8. Add 50-100ml Kefir, try to add this onto the oats
  9. Add milk again, this is so the shake isn't too thick
  10. Add a few icecubes, i like to add about 5 or 6
  11. Close the lid, shake it manually with your hand first to prevent clumping with the peanut butter
  12. After you notice it is somewhat mixed and is not clumped, you are ready to use the blender
  13. Do not rush the blender, mix up the speeds, sometimes go to 0 before turning it on again
  14. Pour into a glass and enjoy
